Analog synth controlled by MIDI input.

MIDI signals are processed by Arduino nano and converted to 1V/Oct signals and mute signals. Three 4-channel DAC provides up to 12 independent voices (VCOs) for flexible sound control. You can choose any VCOs supporting 1V/Oct
